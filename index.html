Introduction To TechCon

Refers to the radio TechCon 2024, the annual UK conference for the audio and rafdio technology community, which took place on November 25, 2024, at IET London: Savoy Place.

©SRC hosts an annual conference for students to showcase their research. The 2024 event highlighted student innovations, and there is a 2025 event for recruiting.
